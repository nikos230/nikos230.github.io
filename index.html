Nikolaos Anastasiou

My diploma thesis focused on the development and evaluation of deep learning models for wildfire spread prediction using remote sensing data. I completed a three-month internship at the National Observatory of Athens, focusing on Earth observation and wildfire mapping. I am currently working at the Athena Research Center, where I am involved in training foundation models for fire detection from satellite data. My primary research interests include remote sensing and deep learning, with an emphasis on wildfire-related applications.
