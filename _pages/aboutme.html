About me

My name is Nikolas Anastasiou. I studied at the School of Rural, Surveying and Geoinformatics Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens. My interests lie in the fields of Remote Sensing, Earth Observation, and Deep Learning.

I have worked on numerous remote sensing projects, primarily involving data from Sentinel-2, Landsat 8/9, and Sentinel-1 satellites. Additionally, I have developed several deep learning models using PyTorch, specifically tailored for processing and analyzing remote sensing data.

My primary interests are in analyzing optical remote sensing data and developing deep learning methods to support and benefit society, as demonstrated in my Diploma Thesis.
